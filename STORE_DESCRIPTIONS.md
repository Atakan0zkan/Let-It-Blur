### EN (English)
🛡️ Let It Blur - Screen Privacy is a fast, ultra-lightweight way to hide your sensitive browser tabs when you step away from your desk.
Whether you're working in a shared office, a cafe, or just need quick privacy from someone walking by, this extension gives you an instant privacy curtain.

What you get:
1️⃣ Instant Privacy: Blur your active web page in a single click without closing your tabs.
2️⃣ Custom Shortcuts: Toggle the blur instantly using `Alt + Shift + X` (or set your own custom key combination).
3️⃣ Smart Auto Away Timer: Forget to blur? The extension automatically secures your screen when you are away from your keyboard for a set amount of time.
4️⃣ Adjustable Visibility: Fine-tune the Blur and Dim intensity to fit your environment through a modern, easy-to-use menu.
5️⃣ Distraction-Free: The blur overlay is completely clean with just a subtle logo—no annoying buttons or text blocking your view.

🔒 No account, no external servers, no page scanning, no personal data collection.

Let It Blur keeps things simple: protect your screen, step away with peace of mind, and get right back to work.
Free to use.
